About me

My name is Ligaya, or you can call me Gaya. I'm currently based in southern Norway and work as a UI/UX Designer at our web developer company. I enjoy creating simple and usable user-interface designs.

I studied UI Design at Careerfoundry and completed the UI Immersion part of the program. I am currently continuing on the specialisation course for Front-end Development.

When I'm not designing, I do gardening, planting plants and flowers and building things. I also like acrylic and alcohol ink painting, and digital and analog photography.
